Phi Lemon is the creative persona of Swiss musician David Zurbuchen, known for his dynamic roles as drummer, guitarist and singer in Switzerland’s jam band scene. After years of ripping it up with bands like Ginger, Marblewood, Utopia in Space, We Believe In Hyperspace, AoxoToxoA, Wua Wua, and others, he writes and records his own songs under the name Phi Lemon. Influenced by the raw energy of 60s garage rock an EP of groovy psychedelic songs is born. Together with longtime friend and collaborator Andreas Rösch, Phi Lemon will take those vibes on an underground club tour in Japan this summer. Back in Switzerland, there will be more shows to come.
